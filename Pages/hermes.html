Apple WATCH | HERMÈS

Design and function.
Inspired by the sea.

Building on a shared foundation of craftsmanship
and beauty, the Apple Watch Hermès portfolio continues to
evolve. The latest collection comprises nautical-inspired
details, luxurious materials, heritage motifs, and Bleu Hydra
En Mer, a new marine blue band for the elegantly durable
Apple Watch Hermès Ultra 2.

Design, set sail. The first Apple Watch Hermès Ultra 2 pairs with the sporty En Mer band, made of water-resistant textured knit in Bleu Hydra or Bleu Nuit that echoes the pattern of ocean waves, featuring a subtle “H” motif and matching custom titanium buckle.

Racing against the clock. Hermès Maritime line is the first-ever digital watch face from the fashion house, featuring graphic Cape Cod numerals. The bezel displays seconds, and with a press of the Action button, a Regatta countdown timer makes keeping time on and off the boat a breeze.
